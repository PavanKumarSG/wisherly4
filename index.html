FAQ

Questions?
We've got answers.

Do I need to create an account?
+

No account needed to get started. Just open the builder, fill in your details, and share the link. Simple as that.

Can the recipient open it on their phone?
+

Absolutely. Every Wisherly surprise page is fully responsive and optimised for mobile. The confetti, animations, and unlock screen all work beautifully on any device.

How do friends add their wishes?
+

You share a separate contributor link with friends. They open it, type their name and message, and it gets added to the page automatically — no account required for them either.

Can I preview the page before sharing?
+

Yes! The builder has a live phone preview on the right side that updates as you type. You can also click "Preview Surprise Page" at any time to see the full experience.

How long does the page stay active?
+

Free pages are stored in the browser locally. Premium and Pro pages are hosted on our servers and remain accessible forever with a shareable link.

Can I add my own music?
+

Yes — paste any YouTube video link and it will appear as a beautiful clickable card on the surprise page. The recipient clicks to open it in YouTube.
